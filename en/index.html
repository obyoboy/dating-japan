About This Site

Who we write for and why

Japan attracts millions of visitors and expats every year — and many fall in love with Japanese culture, and sometimes with Japanese women. Yet navigating the dating scene as a foreigner can be confusing and frustrating without an honest guide.

Dating in Japan Guide fills that gap. We write practical, respectful guides in English for foreign men who are dating — or hoping to date — Japanese women. Our focus is always on building genuine, respectful connections.

Whether you're a tourist, a working expat, or a language student, this site is for you.
